# Daedric Artifacts Tracker

Achievement: **Oblivion Walker**  
Steam text: **Collect 15 Daedric Artifacts.**

This achievement is not just "finish the Daedric quests." Some Daedric quests have a wrong reward that **does not count**.

## Progress

- [x] **Daedric Influence** popped
- [ ] **Oblivion Walker** popped
- Artifact count: `1 / 15`

## Do not count

- **Rueful Axe** — trap reward from **A Daedra's Best Friend**
- **Skeleton Key** — Daedric in lore, not valid for Oblivion Walker
- **Cursed Ring of Hircine** — temporary quest item, not the final artifact
- Random Daedric gear, enchanted loot, or conjured weapons
