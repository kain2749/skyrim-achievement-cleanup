# Thieves Guild Fence Locations

With the main Thieves Guild route and city reputation work complete, the fixed-location fences below should be available. The three caravan fences require Tonilia's **Thieves Guild Caravan Fence Quest**; if that moon sugar delivery was completed, they are also available.

## Fixed-Location Fences

| Fence | City / region | Where to find them |
|---|---|---|
| [Tonilia](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Tonilia) | Riften | Inside **The Ragged Flagon**, reached through the Ratway beneath the city. |
| [Mallus Maccius](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mallus_Maccius) | Whiterun area | Inside **Honningbrew Meadery**, southeast of Whiterun at the road/bridge crossing; usually around the front bar area. |
| [Gulum-Ei](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Gulum-Ei) | Solitude | Inside **The Winking Skeever** near the city entrance, usually in the alcove to the left of the bar. |
| [Enthir](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Enthir) | College of Winterhold | In the **Hall of Attainment**, normally in or near his second-floor quarters; he can sometimes be in The Frozen Hearth in Winterhold. |
| [Endon](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Endon) | Markarth | Often in or near the **Silver-Blood Inn** plaza or on the stone bridge above it; he may also be at **Endon's House**, next to the Treasury House. |
| [Niranye](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Niranye) | Windhelm | At her market stall during business hours, in the market immediately after entering the city; after hours she may be at her nearby house or the New Gnisis Cornerclub. |

## Caravan Fences

These fences move with their caravans and camp outside their endpoint cities. They are available after completing Tonilia's moon sugar delivery to Ri'saad.

| Fence | Caravan route | Where to check |
|---|---|---|
| [Atahbah](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Atahbah) | Whiterun - Markarth | Ri'saad's camp outside either city, near the exterior travel/stable area, or on the road between them. |
| [Zaynabi](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Zaynabi) | Riften - Dawnstar | Ahkari's camp outside **Riften's north gate** or outside Dawnstar, or on the road between them. |
| [Ma'jhad](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Ma%27jhad) | Solitude - Windhelm | Ma'dran's camp outside either city, or on the road between them. |

## Notes

- The restored guild fences generally carry up to `4000` gold, or `5000` with **Master Trader**. **Enthir** is the exception and retains lower merchant gold.
- Mallus and Gulum-Ei have documented fence-service bugs in some game versions. If one refuses to trade, use another available fence rather than repairing quest state unless necessary.
